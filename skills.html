Skills

Scouts

Being a scout added and continues to add many advantages when it comes to skills, from knowing how to handle more outdoors than just walking in a lawn to cut grass, to being able to save someone's life if the situation called for it. Becoming one also requires leading a project which requires, in my case at least, years of planning and effort to pull off, even for something as simple as putting down garden beds and a bench for Fritz Elementary School.

While I tended to be a bit annoying to my fellow scouts, and I still struggle with impulsive behavior today, I have learned to keep a cool head in many situations as well as work with and if needed forgive those who I may not have liked in the past, even if I believe they are still not very mature people, many could say the same about me, so I know it isn't easy to be fully mature.

Academics

While I may not like many of the required academic courses I have been in, a few have showed up as being useful, although not in the way intended, with a cosign math relationship found for animal behavior.
