Projects

Eagle scout

A completion requirement for becoming an eagle scout is to complete a project, the details of which are left open to the scout themselves, besides the rules of it being non-profit and it helping someone, the scout could make bird houses, clean a stream, or, in my case, build garden beds and a bench for a school.

The project first began with assembling the beds after planning and approval from the school and the troup itself, which we did at out house for several hours each day, before painting it and taking it to the school. After finishing that, we brought scouts as well as mulch and stones, and worked for several more hours in the sun to finish, at the end we all had food we had prepared ahead of time for the scouts, and Fritz still uses these beds to this day for their outdoor classroom.
